Walking Tour Website

Background

For my history of Boston class at BU, we were assigned to groups to complete research to then use to create a walking tour around Chelsea Massachusetts. Part of the project was to host this tour online, which I took charge of because of my web dev skillset.

Exploration and Prototyping

The website and our group's written descriptions of each of the tours stops would be beneficial to read during the tour, so I made sure to plan the site to be mobile friendly while still looking good on desktop.

Figma

Development and Result

For the final site, I included alternate historical pictures when reading the descriptions to try to keep walkers engaged, as well as live weather from OpenWeatherMap's API as a way for site visitors to gauge if it's a good time to take the tour.

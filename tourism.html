Tourism Marketing Suite

Background

For my advanced design class at BU, our assignments were structured around one tourist destination of our choice. We then had to create a logo, article, website and app prototype for this chosen destination. I chose Tamarindo, Costa Rica because it was where I took my first solo vacation (which was great), and because I spent a significant amount of time there, so I knew what made sense for the various marketing material I needed to produce.

Exploration and Prototyping

Although I reused assets throughout the projects, I wanted to experiment with different design language for each medium. Each project includes common themes and assets, but they are each meant to stand on their own.

Web Rough App Rough

Development and Result

By the end of the semester I felt that I had a good grasp of the many tools I used to create each piece. Illustrator for the logo, Indesign for the article, HTML, CSS, and JS for the website and Adobe XD for the app prototype.
